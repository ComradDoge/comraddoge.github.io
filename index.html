If VANILLA or SHATTERED is noted before the rule, it means the rule is only specific to that specific version. If there are neither of them that means the rule is in force in all occasions.

I. Miscellaneous

  1. VANILLA: Naval and Maritime Combination is disallowed
  2. VANILLA: Ming is disallowed
  3. Releasing a nation and playing as it is not allowed unless permission is given
  4. Releasing nations in peace deals is disallowed
  5. During the League war, ONLY setting the religion is allowed during the peace deal
  6. The maximum for wars is 25 years after which whatever war score you have, you can force peace for land you occupy.

II. Speed and Lobby

  1. You must be able to play at least speed 2, you will be kicked otherwise.
  2. When you join the lobby, pick your nation and ready up ASAP. Do not be alt-tabbed or clicking on the screen during the loading phase. For superstitious reasons, refrain from typing in chat when syncing up.

III. Subjects

Anbennar: Vassal and PU rules are less strict and for the gamemaster to decide.
  1. If a player is VASSALIZED they have TRUCE(if any)+10 years period to break free (declare war). If they don?t or fail they are considered dead and must leave it.
    1. As the overlord do not enforce a new religion while a player is still present even if it is desired. You are not friends but enemies.
    2. During the peace deal the vassal can only request independence
  2. If a player falls into PERSONAL UNION, they have a TRUCE (if any) +30 year period to break free (declare war). If they don?t or fail they are considered dead and must leave it.
    1. The PU is not to help the overlord in their wars.
    2. The PU does not count to the alliance count
    3. As the overlord do not enforce a new religion while a player is still present even if it is desired. You are not friends but enemies.
    4. During the peace deal the PU can only request independence
  3. Player tributaries do not count as a vassalization, however to tributary someone you need to border them (with a vassal, another tributary or directly).

IV. Diplomacy

  1. Truce breaking and no-CBs against players are not allowed.
  2. No mid-War alliance joining - you are allowed to ally player nations that are at war, but you are not allowed to join their war that has been started before you allied with them.
  3. When is GP status given:
    1. VANILLA: If you are a Holy Roman Emperor as Austria or Bohemia until 1500
    2. If you are 1-8 GP slot in the Great Power list.
4. Player Alliances
SHATTERED: This rule does not come into effect until 1500, due to the quick changing landscape in early game. Until then you are limited to 2 player alliances. Player alliances are limited by the cost of diplomatic relations depending on YOUR country's status. This does not care about any of your AI alliances, only what YOUR empty base number of diplo relations is.
  1. For GPs (1 to 8th) any player alliance will cost 3 diplomatic relations to have 1 player alliance.
  2. For non-GPs (9th to Beyond) any player alliance will cost 2 diplomatic relations to have 1 player alliance.
  3. Colonial Players cost 1 diplomatic relation as subjects until 500 development, after which they cost 2 slots.
  4. Guarantees cost the same as a normal alliance, but only for the guarantor.
Example: YOU are a GP and have 4 (minimum) diplomatic relation slots. A player alliance costs 3 points, meaning YOU can only have 1 player ally because for a second ally you need 3 more points (6 total diplo points) but you only have (4-3) 1 point left. This does not care about any of your AI alliances, only what YOUR empty base number of diplomatic relations is. A non-GP with 4 diplomatic relations is able to get 2 player allies because they only cost 2 each for them. While the other player if they are a GP must consider if they have the points to have them as an ally and if its worth it.

V. Military

  1. Players may not hire or offer condottiere (to players or AIs) to hinder another players war (whether they are fighting against AI or another player) until 1st of January 1500 or until the Italian Wars event starts.
    1. Single regiment to spectate is allowed
    2. Condottieri can only be transported with fleets (transports) that are part of the same war. The enemy should be capable of intercepting the fleet.

Protection

  1. Protection is not automatic and must be requested and granted in the #campaign-changes. Protection will not be granted in the first session no matter the issue. People are free to respect it but they are also free to break it and attack your AI. After 1700 no protection can be given only gentleman's agreement is in place.
  2. If a player nation is protected, any player that gets into a war with that nation must white peace as soon as possible. If any issues arise please note them.
  3. If a protected nation declares an offensive war on another player (because the AI settings weren?t set) they are free to make a peace deal they want but be reasonable if possible
    1. This does not mean that people are allowed to declare them. The country is still protected.

Region System

The world is not fully unlocked at start for play or expansion, it can vary each campaign but here's the general gist of it.
  • Map:
  • Protected regions are not allowed to be expanded into
  • Players who spawned in those regions are protected
  • Protected players can expand outside of protected region and they still cannot be declared on
  • If a protected player declares an offensive player war, land can be taken from them no matter what region it is. Protection is still in place and so unless they declare again you cannot take land.

New World

  1. You can play as a colonial nation (CN) as long as all parties understand what it means.
  2. Requirements for playing CN
    1. Permission from the overlord is given.
    2. Overlord recognizes that they and CN are able to make deals that benefit each other but in the end the CN is capable of betraying them at any point.
  3. CN can only declare when they have 55% or more liberty desire.
  4. If the player CN fails its independence war, it is considered dead and no player can play it for at least 50 years.
  5. Exodus is not allowed.

Respawning

  1. You are allowed to leave your nation at any point, however it will be noted how and why you left. Which may or may not affect decision making.
  2. You can rejoin somewhere else but Admin permission must be given or if a decision is transferred to someone else (by admin) such as the people close to the nation you are choosing.
  3. Respawned and new players can ask the host to change the AI idea set.
    1. Ideas can only be changed to the same monarch category and the player must assign the number of ideas that the new idea group should have so there is no confusion if there are two military idea groups that are not fully filled out.
    2. Government reforms can also be changed but you must write which ones
    3. When someone new joins 15 year protection is in place or until they declare a player offensive war.
      1. If players end up in a war due to AI, the protected player gets the right to request to white peace out.
        1. However, they can also defend AI land and fight the war.
          1. Guaranteed white peace therefore revoked although players can still of course agree to one by normal means.
          2. Only monetary peace deals can be taken from protected player.
    4. Other fixes might occur which will be on the save editors discretion.

Exploits

No exploits or anything that would give you an unfair advantage or would be considered assholery. Includes but is not limited to:
    1. Humiliation or Show of Strength farming between players
    2. Black flagging units to ignore ZoC
    3. Using Neutral Ships to transfer troops
    4. Mothballing the forts of a player which unconditionally surrendered, to allow other players to easily occupy them. Both wars will be annulled and the first player will lose their gains.
    5. Bankruptcy Building
    6. Players COOP'ing and feeding an ally AI to the player enemy
    7. Forcefeeding your AI ally to destabilize them and eat them.
    8. Gaming the coalition system, player declares as coalition leader to white peace and prevent the war for the coalitioned. Face the consequences of your actions.
The admins and mods are free to change these rules during the campaign if its seen that someone acted in bad faith and merely took advantage of a loophole. Whether that person will be penalized will depend on their behaviour and overall actions.
